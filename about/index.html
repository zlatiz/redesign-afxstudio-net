Origins

Venice, CA to
Global Cinema

Born in Venice, California, David's journey into the cinematic unknown began at Santa Monica College before navigating the halls of legendary houses including Stan Winston Studio, ADI, and LAMUD.

It was here that the foundation of physical artistry was laid—where the line between the organic and the engineered first began to blur. His approach is rooted in the classical techniques of physical effects, prioritizing tactile reality over digital artifice.

Leadership

AFX Studio
Command

David leads AFX Studio in partnership with his wife, Heather Langenkamp Anderson. Together, they have cultivated an environment where technological innovation meets classical craftsmanship.

As a prominent member of Local 706 and having served 20 years on the Academy Make-Up Selection Committee, David continues to shape the future of the industry while honoring its rich history.
